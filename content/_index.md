## I'm Kenneth, and I build things that matter.

A billion people will be able to build software within the decade. Almost none of them will know how to keep it running. Building is getting easier every day — running what you've built is not. That gap is the bottleneck for the next era of economic growth.

I'm building [OpsCompanion](https://opscompanion.ai/) to close it. If we get this right, we don't just make operations easier — we unlock the full potential of a world where anyone can ship software that actually works.

Before this, I spent years in the trenches — infrastructure at Cloudflare, AI-driven platforms at Balto, advising startups, and doing technical due diligence for VCs. I know what breaks at scale because I've been the one paged at 3am to fix it.
